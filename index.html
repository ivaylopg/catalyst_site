The Catalyst

The Catalyst is a group of scientific researchers using theatre as a means of engagement with the public. We believe combining science and theatre becomes valuable when:

theatre skills (link to workshops) empower scientists to present their work to their colleagues in an engaging and productive manner;

theatrical experience (link to workshops) helps scientists to communicate new research and illuminate the process of scientific reasoning for the general public;

creating new theatre about scientific research (link to projects) publicizes unique narratives and genuine characters from the scientific culture. Scientists who collaborate with theatre professionals can connect passionately with the public while maintaining scientific integrity.
